📜 My Content Journey

My journey as a creator started with a simple observation: the intersection of technology and creativity is where magic happens. While most people see a portfolio, I see a playground for innovation.

🚀 The Early Days

I began as a CS student fascinated by AI and NLP. My AI Quiz Generator wasn't just a project—it was my first "aha!" moment. Realizing I could create tools that think? That changed everything. I started documenting this journey on Instagram, sharing not just code, but the thought process behind it.

📱 The Full-Stack Evolution

Then came the Android app. Real-time social media, WebRTC, Firebase—I wasn't just building anymore; I was architecting. I discovered that product thinking matters as much as coding. This led me to roles as a Scrum master and Product Owner, reshaping how I approach problems.

🎮 Playing with Games

C++ and SFML brought me joy. Creating Centipede and Xonic weren't about nostalgia—they were about understanding physics, collision detection, and the craft of interactive experiences. Games taught me that every pixel matters.

🧠 AI & The Future

Today, I'm obsessed with intelligent systems. NLP, machine learning, creative problem-solving—these aren't buzzwords. They're the lens through which I see the world. I believe technology should feel alive, useful, and yes, a little strange.

And that's why this portfolio has a cat. Because sometimes, the best way to be memorable is to be authentically you. 🐱
